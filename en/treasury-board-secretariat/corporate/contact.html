Helpful resource(s):

If you require technical assistance, please contact your department help desk. (e.g. unable to get to PRI validation, getting an error when trying to do an action in the application).

If you have questions related to your employment status or your employee file, please contact your manager and/or your department’s human resources unit.

If you need business assistance, please contact your department’s human resources unit. (e.g. unable to find an employee, entered incorrect information, need to update your information or have program or policy questions).

For questions related to performance management or the Public Service Performance Management Application (PSPM App) in the TBS Applications Portal (TAP), send an email to Questions@tbs-sct.gc.ca.
