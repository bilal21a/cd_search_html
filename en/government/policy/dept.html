A - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter A

Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada
Describes acts and regulations related to agriculture and food in Canada.
Atlantic Pilotage Authority
Includes the Pilotage Act, pilotage regulations, APA regulations, non-compulsory area regulations and tariff regulations.

B - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter B

Buyandsell.gc.ca
Includes Government of Canada procurement policy and guidelines and supply and acquisition manuals.

C - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter C

Canada Border Service Agency
Includes the Customs Act, the customs tariff and other regulatory information.
Canada Council for the Arts
Includes the Canada Council for the Arts Act.
Canada School of Public Service
Includes the Canada School of the Public Service Act.
Canadian Broadcasting Corporation
Includes programming policies, the Official Languages Act, management policies and corporate by-laws.
Canadian Coast Guard
Includes acts, regulations and service standards for high-volume regulatory authorizations.
Canadian Dairy Commission
Includes marketing and export regulations and the Canadian Dairy Commission Act.
Canadian Environmental Assessment Agency
Includes regulations related to environmental assessments in Canada, their funding, and designated projects.
Canadian Food Inspection Agency
Includes food safety, inspection, animal health, seeds and food labelling requirements and regulations.
Canadian Heritage
Includes acts regarding cultural protection, cultural property and Canadian audio-visual productions.
Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission
Includes nuclear security, safety and control.
Canadian Pari-Mutuel Agency
Includes the regulatory framework for betting on horse racing in Canada.
Canadian Polar Commission
Includes the act establishing the commission to develop and disseminate information about the Polar Regions.
Canadian Transportation Agency
Includes acts and regulations for transportation carriers under federal jurisdiction.
Citizenship and Immigration Canada
Includes the Citizenship Act, the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, and the Canadian Multiculturalism Act.
Commission for Public Complaints Against the RCMP
Includes access to information requests, as well as acts and regulations such as the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Act.
Copyright Board of Canada
Includes the Copyright Act and Copyright Regulations.
Correctional Service of Canada
Includes the Corrections and Conditional Release Act and regulations, the Safe Streets and Communities Act, Commissioner's Directives and standard operating practices.

E - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter E

Employment and Social Development Canada
Includes acts and regulations relating to the labour market.
Environment and Climate Change Canada
Includes the Canadian Environmental Protection Act, Environmental Registry, and the Species at Risk public registry.

F - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter F

Farm Products Council of Canada
Includes acts regarding farm and agricultural products.
Department of Finance Canada
Includes acts and regulations regarding the government's management of the economy, banks, and taxes.
Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre of Canada
Includes regulations under the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act.
Fisheries and Oceans Canada
Includes acts and regulations relating to marine fisheries, aquatic species and more.
Foreign Affairs, Trade and Development Canada
Includes policies, guidelines and key information for the diplomatic community.

G - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter G

Great Lakes Pilotage Authority
Includes general pilotage regulations as well as great lakes pilotage regulations and tariff regulations.

H - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter H

Health Canada
Includes acts and regulations relating to the health and safety of Canadians.

I - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter I

Industry Canada
Includes acts and regulations related to the economy, telecommunications, competition, the marketplace and consumers.
Invest in Canada
Includes rules for foreign companies seeking to do business in Canada .

L - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter L

Labour Program
Includes acts and regulations relating to labour laws for federally regulated employers and workers of federally-regulated organizations.
Laurentian Pilotage Authority
Includes regulations for such topics as public ports and public port facilities.

M - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter M

Military Grievances External Review Committee
Includes the National Defence Act and the Queen's Regulations and Orders for the Canadian Forces.
Military Police Complaints Commission
Includes regulations under the National Defence Act that are relevant to the commission's activities.

N - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter N

The National Battlefields Commission
Contains the Act respecting the National Battlefields at Quebec, governing sites including the Plains of Abraham.
National Defence
Includes policies and standards related to defence and the military.
National Energy Board
Includes acts, regulations, rules and guidance notes that the Board operates under.
Natural Resources Canada
Includes acts, regulations activities relating to information such as Service Standards and Departmental actions.
Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada
Includes policies and guidelines intended for professors, students and fellows, institutions, College and Community Innovation Program.

O - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter O

Office of Energy Efficiency
Includes regulations, standards and their amendments on energy efficient homes and products, automobiles, fuels, industrial facilities and equipment and communities and government.
Office of the Procurement Ombudsman
Includes acts, regulations and information on accountabilities in the areas of finance, procurement, contracting and land claims.

P - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter P

Parks Canada
Includes policies, regulations and laws about Canada’s Parks.
Parole Board of Canada
Includes the legislative framework which guides PBC policies, operations, training and parole decision making and the legal framework for the correctional and parole system in Canada.
Patented Medicine Prices Review Board
Includes acts, regulations and guidelines on patented medicines and prices of patented drug products.
Public Health Agency of Canada
Includes acts and regulations associated with the Public Health Agency of Canada, as well as information on regulatory initiatives, and related sites.
Public Safety Canada
Includes acts and regulations, some administered by Public Safety Canada (PS) and others administered by agencies within the Public Safety Portfolio.
Public Service Commission of Canada
Includes acts, regulations and orders as well as the forward regulatory plan under the Commission's responsibility.
Public Service Staffing Tribunal
Includes the Public Service Employment Act and Public Service Staffing Tribunal regulations.
Public Works and Government Services Canada
Includes acts and regulations related to the department's role as the government's principal banker, accountant, central purchasing agent, linguistic authority, and real property manager.

R - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter R

Royal Canadian Mounted Police
Includes the Royal Canada Mounted Police Act, RCMP regulations, Commissioner's Standing Orders, Canadian Firearms Program and vulnerable sector checks.

S - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter S

Statistics Canada
Includes acts and regulations related to statistical information to ensure that Canadians can access a trusted source of statistics on Canada to meet their highest priority needs.
Supreme Court of Canada
Includes the Supreme Court Act, the Rules of the Supreme Court of Canada and guidelines on how to fill out court documents.

T - Government of Canada regulations, policies and laws by department or agency beginning with the letter T

Transport Canada
Includes acts, regulations and related bills for air, marine, rail and road transportation.
Transportation Appeal Tribunal of Canada
Includes to the Transportation Appeal Tribunal of Canada Rules, effective June 1, 1986.
Transportation Safety Board of Canada
Includes acts, regulations, standards and policies that help ensure that transportation investigations are conducted in a systematic, thorough, and unbiased manner.
Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat
Includes acts and regulations related to the Secretariat's financial, human resources and administrative responsibilities.
