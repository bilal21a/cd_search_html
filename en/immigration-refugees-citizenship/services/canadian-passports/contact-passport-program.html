Contact us about your refugee travel document or certificate of identity

Contact the IRCC Client Support Centre:

  • IRCC Web form
  • Telephone: 1-888-242-2100 (in Canada only)

    • Automated telephone service (available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week)

      If you have a touch-tone telephone you can listen to pre-recorded information about our programs, and check the status of your application.

    • Client Support Centre agents - Monday to Friday, 8 am to 4 pm, your local time, except for statutory holidays. Services are available in French and English.
  • If you are hearing impaired, or you have a speech impediment and use a text telephone, you can access the TTY service from 8 am to 4 pm your local time by calling 1-888-576-8502.
