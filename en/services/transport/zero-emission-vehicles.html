Choose a zero-emission vehicle

More Canadians and Canadian businesses each year are switching to zero-emission vehicles (ZEVs), like electric vehicles, taking advantage of the many economic and environmental benefits and the rapidly growing selection of makes and models.

How can I save?

Apply for incentives towards the purchase of ZEVs. Save even more with the lower costs of electric vehicle charging, compared to fuelling with gas.

Learn more about the incentives for purchasing a ZEV

Funding for infrastructure and awareness projects

Funding for projects that enable the ZEV transition, including the development and installation of reliable ZEV charging and refuelling infrastructure, and awareness and education initiatives to drive greater confidence and adoption.

Learn more about ZEV funding programs

Greening commercial vehicles and fleets

Funding programs and incentives to help businesses and governments make the transition to ZEVs.

Learn more about greening fleets

Leading the charge

With a federal commitment to achieve 100% new light-duty zero-emission vehicles sales by 2035, Canada is becoming a global leader in the transition to ZEVs.
