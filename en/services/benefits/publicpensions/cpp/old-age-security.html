Old Age Security

The Old Age Security (OAS) pension is a monthly payment you can get if you are 65 and older.

In most cases, Service Canada will be able to automatically enroll you for the OAS pension. Service Canada will inform you if you have been automatically enrolled.

You may have to apply for the Old Age Security pension if we do not have enough information to enroll you automatically.
