Contact Us

Have you received a call from 613-993-9620 or another phone number listed on our website?  These are public phone numbers for CSIS, and they have been spoofed.  Calls from these phone numbers asking for social insurance numbers or financial information are a scam.  We encourage Canadians to report such instances to the Canadian Antifraud Centre by phone at 1-888-495-8501 or here: http://www.antifraudcentre-centreantifraude.ca/index-eng.htm

If you require immediate assistance, please call 9-1-1.
