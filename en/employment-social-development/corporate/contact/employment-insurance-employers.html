Employment Insurance information for employers

Online

You can complete the following tasks online:

  • issue your records of employment (ROE)
  • apply for a Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) using the LMIA Online portal
  • download the Supplemental Unemployment Benefit plan registration form
  • view the Supplemental Unemployment Benefit program guide
  • view the Employment Insurance (EI) Premium Reduction guide
  • download the EI Premium Reduction application form

Employer Contact Centre

You can contact the Employer Contact Centre in order to obtain:

  • advice and guidance about issuing records of employment (ROE)
  • ordering paper ROE forms
  • technical support for ROE Web
  • registration support for Grants and Contributions Online Services (GCOS)
  • Data Gateway support for:
    • Automated Earnings Reporting System (AERS)
    • Report on Hirings (RoH)
    • Temporary Foreign Worker Program’s Global Talent Stream
    • Work-Sharing Program
  • information about the Temporary Foreign Worker Program (TFWP)
  • information about Job Bank for Employers in Quebec
  • information on the Work-Sharing Program
  • information on the EI Premium Reduction Program (PRP)
  • information on the Supplemental Unemployment Benefit (SUB) program

For other enquiries about EI, you can visit Employment Insurance benefits.
