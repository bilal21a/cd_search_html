Wildfires

In Canada, emergencies are managed first at the local and then at the provincial or territorial level; if they need help, they can ask the federal government for more resources. The Government of Canada has programs, policies, and initiatives to keep Canadians informed about the wildfire situation and to support provincial and territorial efforts to combat wildfires.

Most requested

  • Provincial and territorial wildfire information
  • Fire maps (Canadian Wildland Fire Information System)
  • Wildfire smoke, air quality and your health
  • Smoke maps
Wildfire Donation-Matching Initiatives

If you are interested in making a donation to help those affected by wildfires in the Northwest Territories, or Nova Scotia, please visit the Wildfire Donation-Matching Initiatives page to learn about how your donation could be tripled.
