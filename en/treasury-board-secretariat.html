Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat

The Secretariat ensures tax dollars are spent wisely and effectively for Canadians. We oversee and provide guidance to the Treasury Board of Ministers on how government is managed and how it regulates. We lead on digital transformation, and employ a diverse and inclusive public service.
