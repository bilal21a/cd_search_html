Services and information

Youth jobs and internships

Travel and work in different regions across Canada, develop new skills, establish contacts in your field of study and boost your career possibilities, all while having loads of fun and adventure.

Youth shaping Canada

Have a voice in what matters to young Canadians. Engage your community and share your perspectives, apply to the Prime Minister's Youth Council, check out what happened at the Canada Youth Summit, and learn about how you can get involved.

Exchange and leadership programs

Develop your leadership skills, participate in exchanges and dive in to youth-led projects. A new academic adventure is waiting for you in unique Canadian communities.

Language immersion programs

Take part in a language bursary or work experience program to strengthen your first or second language, and at the same time, get to learn about a new part of Canada and its culture.

Get involved with  Parks Canada

Find out about youth programs and activities in Canada’s national historic sites, national parks and national marine conservation areas.

Cadets and Junior Canadian Rangers

These funded programs for youth aged 12 to 18 teach valuable life skills such as teamwork and leadership through fun and rewarding activities.
