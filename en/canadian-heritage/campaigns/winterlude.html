Winterlude 2024

Winterlude is the can’t-miss winter event in Canada’s Capital Region!

From February 2 to 19, Ottawa and Gatineau will be bustling with a host of indoor and outdoor activities that showcase Indigenous culture, Canadian diversity and significant anniversaries. Whether you enjoy the outdoors or prefer to take in a show or exhibit, Winterlude has you covered with more that 200 activities.
