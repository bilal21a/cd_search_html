Military pay

Pay rates for all ranks, occupations, and types of service in the Canadian Army, Royal Canadian Air Force, Royal Canadian Navy, and Special Forces.

Services and information

Regular Force pay by rank

Pay rates for full-time Regular Force and Reserve Class C service. Includes privates, corporals, sergeants, captains, and senior officers.

Reserve Force pay by rank

Pay rates for part-time Reserve Class A and Class B service. Includes privates, corporals, sergeants, captains, and senior officers.

Understanding military pay

Trade groups and factors that determine pay for all ranks and occupations in the Canadian Armed Forces.

Pay statements

Electronic copies of notices that come with your pay. Terms and acronyms on your pay statement.

Canadian Forces severance pay

Severance pay is calculated based on your years of eligible service.

Previous military pay rates

Previous daily and monthly pay scales for the Canadian Armed Forces, starting as early as October 1970.
