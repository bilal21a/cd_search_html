GATS countries - You may be eligible to work under a free trade agreement

Canada is part of 1 free trade agreement with this country or territory:

  • General Agreement on Trade in Services (GATS)

CETA countries - You may be eligible to work under a free trade agreement

Canada is part of 2 free trade agreements with this country:

  • Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement (CETA)
  • General Agreement on Trade in Services (GATS)

United States - You may be eligible to work under a free trade agreement

  • Canada-United States-Mexico Agreement (CUSMA)
  • General Agreement on Trade in Services (GATS)

Specialists

As a specialist, you must have:

  • knowledge at an advanced level of expertise and
  • proprietary knowledge of your company’s
    • product
    • service
    • research
    • equipment
    • techniques or
    • management

How to apply for a work permit

Countries participating in the CPTPP

To be eligible, you must be a citizen of one of the following:

  • Australia (or permanent resident)
  • Brunei
  • Chile
  • Japan
  • Malaysia
  • Mexico
  • New Zealand (or permanent resident)
  • Peru
  • Singapore
  • Vietnam

Multinational company

This is any corporation that is registered and operates in more than 1 country at a time.

Investor

CETA defines an investor as a manager or specialist who:

  • is responsible for setting up an enterprise but
  • is not paid by a Canadian source
  • doesn’t have direct transactions with general public

Investor

CUKTCA defines an investor as a manager or specialist who:

  • is responsible for setting up an enterprise but
  • is not paid by a Canadian source
  • does not engage in direct transactions with general public
Graduate trainee

CETA defines a graduate trainee as someone temporarily transferred to an enterprise in Canada to:

  • develop their career or
  • obtain business training

Specialized knowledge worker

As a specialized knowledge worker, you must have:

  • knowledge at an advanced level of expertise and
  • proprietary knowledge of your company’s
    • product
    • service
    • research
    • equipment
    • techniques or management

How to apply for a work permit

Trainees on professional development

Trainees are:

  • employees with a post-secondary degree
  • on a temporary work assignment
  • getting more experience in the company to prepare for a senior position

How to apply for a work permit

General service business activities

These business activities include:

  • commercial transactions by managers or financial services personnel
  • public relations
  • advertising
  • tourism personnel
  • translation and interpretation
  • cook personnel attending or participating in a culinary event, or consulting with business associates
  • IT service providers attending meetings or consultations
  • franchise traders and developers seeking to offer their services
