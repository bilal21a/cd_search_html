Contact the Labour Program

Note: To report a serious injury, death or refusal to work, call us toll-free at 1-800-641-4049 (emergency services 24 hours).

If you have questions about federally regulated employment standards, health and safety, workplace equality, employment equity, or workers' compensation that are not answered on our Web site:

  • Contact the nearest office of the Labour Program by telephone or mail. You can also send us an email using our online form by choosing one of the following topics:
    • Employer's Annual Hazardous Occurrence Report (EAHOR) - contact form
    • Employment equity - contact form
    • Employment standards - contact form
    • Federal mediation and conciliation service - contact form
    • Federal Workers' Compensation Service
    • Harassment and Violence Prevention - contact form
    • Health and safety - contact form
    • Labour Funding program - contact form
    • Workplace information division
  • For all enquiries about Workers' compensation, please contact the Federal Workers' Compensation Service.
