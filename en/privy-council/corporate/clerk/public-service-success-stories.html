Public service stories


Featured stories

Every day, public servants accomplish great things to build a strong and modern public service, provide better services to Canadians, and apply innovative solutions to address challenges in Canada and around the world. We bring our diverse sets of skills and knowledge to reflect the unique experience of this country. These are our stories - by Canadians, for Canadians.


Overcoming personal struggles and helping others

Daniel Gervais talks about how his personal journey dealing with disability and mental health issues has led him to advocate for persons with disabilities at the Canadian Food Inspection Agency.

Providing IT to the Canadian High Arctic Research Station Campus

A cutting-edge research station up north is strengthening Canada’s place as a polar research leader. We made sure the station’s IT infrastructure will help it do just that.

In My Own Words: Yueshuang Zhu

Yueshuang Zhu, a Project Manager for the Parliamentary Precinct Centre Block Program, talks about his work in ensuring that Centre Block building, the iconic structure at the heart of Canadian democracy, is ready to undergo rehabilitation.
