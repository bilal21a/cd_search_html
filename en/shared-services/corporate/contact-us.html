Contact Shared Services Canada

Have you received a call from 613-670-1627?

This is the phone number for Shared Services Canada Public Enquiries and it is being spoofed. No representative from Shared Services Canada is trying to reach you. Fraudsters are masking their phone number with ours.

When you miss a call and the caller doesn’t leave a message, don’t return the call. If it’s important, they’ll call you back. If it’s a long distance number, you could be charged long distance fees.

If you have doubts about the validity of communications received from the Government of Canada, please contact 1-800-OCANADA. You can also report fraudulent calls to the Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre online or by calling 1-888-495-8501.

 

Shared Services Canada welcomes your questions, comments and suggestions.

If you are looking for information related to programs such as Old Age Security (OAS), Employment Insurance,  Canada Pension Plan (CPP), passport, Social Insurance Number (SIN), or the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB), please visit Service Canada, or by calling 1-800-OCANADA (1-800-622-6232).
