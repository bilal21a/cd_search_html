Skills for Success – Assessment and training tools

From: Employment and Social Development Canada

Find the right tools to assess or upgrade your skills. Most of these tools are free for individuals. New Skills for Success tools will be added regularly as they are developed.

If you are an employer, training provider or career counsellor looking for tools to support your clients or learners, set the filter below, left to “I am an… employer” instead of individual. Some of these tools have a cost for you to use them, as noted in the description.

The tools listed are from the Government of Canada and outside organizations. Organizations that wish to have their tool(s) included, may view the criteria to apply.

For additional information on the skills visit the Learn about the Skills page.

Skills for Success Mobile App
Assessment
Individual

A comprehensive set of random questions that can be completed on average in 15-20 minutes for each of the nine skills for success. Results are provided as a star rating link to careers in trade and technology sectors where the star rating represents complexity levels of tasks associated with the particular skills for success being assessed. Most questions will test skill levels from one to three.

Cost: No

Workplace Digital Skills
Training
Individual

The Workplace Digital Skills Training is an online training tool designed to increase the basic digital skills of workers and job seekers. It includes 4 separate workshops (8 hours of online learning in total) and is designed for learners to follow on their own. This training will be free for the first 200 registered learners as part of a pilot project funded by Employment and Social Development Canada.

Cost: Free for first 200 registered learners
