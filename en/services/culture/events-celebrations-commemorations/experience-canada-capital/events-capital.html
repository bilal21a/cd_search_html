Services and information

Canada Day

Take part in the nation's biggest birthday party on July 1. Witness the shows and fireworks and take in the musical talent at several locations in Canada’s Capital Region.

Winterlude

Join us at Winterlude, the Capital's winter celebration. Skate on the world's largest skating rink or play at North America's largest snow playground.

Winter Lights Across Canada 

Experience the hundreds of thousands of multi-coloured lights that brighten the national capital and other wintery capitals across Canada during the holiday season.

Tulips in Canada's capital

Catch the dazzling display of one million tulips in parks and gardens across Canada’s Capital Region.

Volunteer Centre

Register to be a volunteer at one of the many events and celebrations in Canada’s Capital Region, including Canada Day and Winterlude.

Capital Experience Events Newsletter

Receive communications by email about the events organized in Canada’s Capital region.
