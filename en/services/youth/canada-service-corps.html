2022-09-16

The Canada Service Corps is a national movement to build a culture of volunteer service that empowers young Canadians to make an impact.

The Prime Minister and Minister of Youth, Justin Trudeau, launched the Canada Service Corps, Canadas new national youth service initiative on January 16, 2018. Young Canadians can volunteer for the Canada Service Corps by applying at youth.volunteer.ca.

Young Canadians can volunteer for the Canada Service Corps by applying at youth.volunteer.ca.

Canada Service Corps will be provided with $105 million up to March 2021, and will fund eligible projects that present opportunities for young Canadians to get involved in service to communities.

The Canada Service Corps helps young Canadians between the ages of 15-30 find volunteer opportunities within their communities. Visit Canada.ca to learn more.

Volunteering builds your skills, accomplishments, interests and values, which are the foundation of career success. The Canada Service Corps program helps young people between the ages of 15-30 get meaningful service experience.
